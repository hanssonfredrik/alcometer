Neon Tracker

Privacy & Support

Effective 11 July 2026 · applies to the web app and the iOS/Android apps (se.venueve.neontracker)
Neon Tracker collects nothing. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no transmission of your data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

What is stored, and where

The app stores only what you enter to make the tracker work: your drink log, your profile (weight and sex, used for the blood-alcohol estimate), your drink-size presets, and nightly limits.

  • On the web/PWA, this lives in your browser's local storage for this site.
  • On iOS and Android, it lives in the app's private on-device storage.

Nothing is sent to a server. We operate no backend and hold no copy of your data — we could not access, share, or sell it even if asked.

Deleting your data

  • iOS / Android: uninstall the app. All data is removed with it.
  • Web: clear this site's browsing data in your browser settings.

There is nothing to delete anywhere else — no server copies exist.

Third parties

None. The app embeds no third-party analytics, advertising, or social SDKs, and makes no runtime network requests with your data.

Health disclaimer

Blood-alcohol values shown by the app are estimates based on the Widmark formula. They vary from your actual level and must never be used to decide whether it is safe to drive, operate machinery, or anything else. When in doubt, don't.

Support & contact
